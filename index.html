Privacy Policy


Galacticware Keyboards builds software keyboards for purchase and all products are intended for use as is. This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

Information Collection and Use

The app does NOT collect any personal information, log any keystrokes, or save any data beyond to remote locations, or use data beyond the baunds of the application itself. Local data is store for clipboard entries are not saved (this data is erased when the app stops running). Macros are saved to a local, in-app database. This app does not use internet access.
The app does NOT use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.
